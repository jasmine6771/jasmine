ABOUT


LoThesa's Place, nestled in Sitio Puro Tugas, Barangay Calumpang, Naval, Biliran, offers a serene escape. Immerse yourself in nature's beauty, relax in our peaceful hillside setting, and rejuvenate your senses. Our lush greenery, vibrant flowers, and towering trees create a tranquil atmosphere. Unwind on our Instagram-worthy Nest Swing, surrounded by breathtaking views. Indulge in delicious refreshments that nourish both body and soul.


Information


Surround yourself with nature, relax in the peaceful hillside setting with breathtaking views, and indulge in delicious refreshments that rejuvenate both body and soul.

LoThesa's Place boasts lush greenery, vibrant flowers, and towering trees, creating a peaceful atmosphere perfect for unwinding. The soothing sounds of nature and gentle breezes will calm your senses, while the picturesque surroundings will inspire your creativity.

The Nest Swing is a distinctive, Instagram-worthy spot, perfect for photo ops. Surrounded by lush greenery, the swing offers a peaceful, relaxing experience.
